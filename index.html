Who We Are

A community-rooted nonprofit for a more compassionate city.

Compassionate St. Augustine is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit working with neighbors, volunteers, businesses, faith communities, and local partners to make compassion visible in daily life.

Through advocacy, awareness, and action, CSA helps people care for one another across generations, backgrounds, and neighborhoods.
