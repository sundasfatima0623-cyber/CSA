Who We Are

Compassionate St. Augustine (CSA) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit rooted in the belief that every neighborhood can become more caring, connected, and equitable. We bring residents, volunteers, businesses, and public partners together to turn compassion into practical action. From food access to advocacy and elder support, CSA helps neighbors show up for one another.
