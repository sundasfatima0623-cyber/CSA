Who We Are

Rooted in St. Augustine.
Growing Compassion.

Compassionate St. Augustine (CSA) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to influencing and growing a culture of compassion throughout St. Augustine, Florida. We believe that compassion is not a passive feeling but an active, community-wide commitment.

Through partnerships with local leaders, businesses, youth, and neighbors, CSA weaves a fabric of care and advocacy — from community gardens and women's circles to grassroots activism and support for our aging residents.

We are part of the global Charter for Compassion movement, bringing its principles home to the ancient city we love.
