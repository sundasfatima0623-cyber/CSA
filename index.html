Who We Are

A community rooted in compassion

Compassionate St. Augustine is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to building a kinder, more inclusive city. We bring neighbors, organizations, and leaders together — across generations and backgrounds — to act with empathy and create meaningful change in St. Augustine, Florida.
