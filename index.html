Who We Are

Neighbors building a more compassionate St. Augustine

Compassionate St. Augustine (CSA) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit rooted in the belief that a caring city grows through shared responsibility, everyday kindness, and courageous advocacy. We convene residents, nonprofits, businesses, faith leaders, and public partners to strengthen connection across neighborhoods and create practical pathways for people to help one another.

From food security and business recognition to women’s leadership, civic action, and aging support, our programs invite the whole community to participate in compassion that is visible, organized, and lasting.
