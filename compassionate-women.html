Compassionate Women

Stronger Together

Compassionate Women fosters inclusivity and compassion among women of all ethnicities and backgrounds, with a focus on supporting children and adult residents of West Augustine.

Join Compassionate Women

Our Focus

Women leading with listening, care, and action.

The group creates welcoming circles where women build friendships, learn from one another, and respond to real needs in the community.

  • Inclusive gatherings across age, race, faith, and life experience.
  • Support for children, families, and adult residents in West Augustine.
  • Shared service projects that turn compassion into practical help.

Community Impact in West Augustine

Standing beside neighbors where support is needed most.

Compassionate Women works with residents and community partners to strengthen belonging, opportunity, and dignity.

🎒

Children and youth

Support for school readiness, mentoring relationships, and positive neighborhood activities.

🤝

Adult residents

Connection to local resources, neighborly assistance, and encouragement through difficult seasons.

🕊️

Inclusive belonging

Gatherings that help women know one another, honor lived experience, and practice compassion.

Join Us

Bring your story, your gifts, and your heart.
