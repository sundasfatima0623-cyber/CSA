Our Focus

Every Woman Belongs Here

Compassionate Women is a CSA initiative built on one simple truth: when women are supported, whole communities thrive. Our circle welcomes women of every ethnicity, background, age, and circumstance — because compassion has no boundaries.

Our key focus is supporting children and adult residents of West Augustine — one of St. Augustine's most historically underserved neighborhoods — through mentorship, resource connections, and sisterhood.

We meet, we listen, we learn, and we act. From community dinners to advocacy efforts, every gathering is a chance to strengthen the bonds that make St. Augustine stronger.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

💜

Inclusivity

Every woman is welcome — regardless of race, religion, age, income, or background. Our diversity is our greatest strength.

🤝

Mutual Support

We show up for each other. Whether navigating personal challenges or community crises, no woman stands alone in this circle.

🌱

Empowerment

We lift each other up — connecting women to resources, opportunities, education, and leadership that help them and their families flourish.

🎤

Advocacy

We use our collective voice to advocate for equity, justice, and dignity — for the women in our circle and for all residents of St. Augustine.

Community Impact

Making a Difference in West Augustine

West Augustine has historically been overlooked. Compassionate Women is committed to changing that — one connection, one resource, one act of care at a time.

👧

Children's Support

After-school programs, mentoring relationships, and resource connections for the children of West Augustine — investing in the next generation.

🏠

Adult Resident Resources

Connecting adult residents to social services, housing resources, employment opportunities, and community support networks.

🍽️

Community Dinners

Regular community meals where neighbors come together, relationships are built, and no one eats alone. Food is love made visible.

📚

Education & Workshops

Skills workshops, health education, and leadership development opportunities designed for and led by women in the community.
