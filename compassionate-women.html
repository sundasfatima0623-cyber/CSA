Our Focus

Inclusivity is the heart of our work

Compassionate Women fosters genuine connection and compassion among women of all ethnicities, ages, and backgrounds. Together, we listen, mentor, organize, and show up — for one another and for our neighbors.

Our shared focus is supporting the children and adult residents of West Augustine — meeting real needs, celebrating real progress, and building enduring relationships across our community.

Community Impact

In service of West Augustine

From classroom drives to neighbor-to-neighbor mentoring, our work centers the residents who know West Augustine best.

📚

Children & Schools

Backpack drives, reading partnerships, and after-school encouragement for West Augustine students.

🤝

Mentorship

One-on-one mentorship that walks alongside women navigating new chapters in life.

🌷

Wellness Circles

Quiet spaces for connection, healing, and shared strength among women of every generation.

🍲

Neighborhood Care

Meal trains, ride-shares, and welcome baskets for adult residents of West Augustine.
