What Is the Purple Bench?

A Symbol of Compassionate Commerce

The Purple Bench is CSA's recognition program for local St. Augustine businesses that demonstrate our core values in the way they operate, treat their employees, serve their customers, and show up for the community.

Named for the purple bench — a universal symbol of welcome and inclusion — this program celebrates businesses that prove you don't have to choose between doing well and doing good.

Certified Purple Bench businesses display our signature window cling, distinguishing themselves to customers who want to spend their dollars with compassionate businesses.

💜 Empathy 📣 Advocacy 🤝 Social Responsibility 🌿 Sustainability 🔗 Collaboration
How to Qualify

The Certification Process

Earning the Purple Bench designation is a meaningful process that reflects genuine commitment — not just a checkbox exercise.

1

Nomination

Any community member can nominate a local business they believe embodies CSA's core values. Self-nominations are also welcome.

2

Application & Review

Nominated businesses complete a short application describing their compassionate practices. CSA's review committee evaluates submissions holistically.

3

Community Verification

We reach out to employees, customers, and community partners to verify that the business's practices align with their stated values.

4

Recognition & Celebration

Certified businesses receive their recognition package and are celebrated at our annual awards luncheon — a highlight of St. Augustine's civic calendar.
