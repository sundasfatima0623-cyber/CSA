Compassion in Business

The Purple Bench honors local St. Augustine businesses that embody empathy, advocacy, sustainability, and collaboration in everyday operations.

What Is the Purple Bench?

The Purple Bench is CSA's way of celebrating businesses that choose purpose alongside profit. Honorees are visible leaders for compassionate business practices and inspire others to follow.

How to Qualify

Businesses can qualify by demonstrating:

  • Policies and practices that prioritize empathy and inclusion
  • Community advocacy or direct support for local families
  • Environmentally responsible operations and partnerships
  • Collaborative efforts that strengthen neighborhoods and nonprofits

Recognized Businesses

CSA showcases Purple Bench honorees in community communications and events to spotlight their leadership and impact.

Donor Tier 1: Window Cling

A visible symbol of commitment to compassion that customers and neighbors can see at every entrance.

Donor Tier 2: Bench with Plaque

Sponsor a Purple Bench installation with a recognition plaque and help create lasting community spaces.

Donor Tier 3: Awards Luncheon Sponsorship

Support the annual Purple Bench awards gathering celebrating compassionate business champions.
