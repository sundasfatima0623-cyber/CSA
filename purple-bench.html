The Idea

What is the Purple Bench?

The Purple Bench is CSA’s recognition program for local businesses that quietly, consistently embody our core values. Inspired by the simple idea of a bench out front — a place to rest, connect, and feel welcome — the program celebrates businesses that put people first.

Recognized businesses receive a window cling, a public listing, and an invitation to our annual awards luncheon.

Our Core Values

How to Qualify

Recognized businesses demonstrate, in real and observable ways, the values at the heart of CSA.

💜

Empathy

Treating staff, customers, and neighbors with genuine care.

📣

Advocacy

Lifting up local causes and amplifying neighborhood voices.

🌎

Social Responsibility

Investing in the well-being of the wider St. Augustine community.

♻️

Sustainability

Operating with care for the environment and future generations.

🤝

Collaboration

Partnering with other businesses, nonprofits, and neighbors.
