The Purple Bench

Compassion in Business

The Purple Bench recognizes local St. Augustine businesses that exhibit CSA's core values: empathy, advocacy, social responsibility, sustainability, and collaboration.

Nominate a Business

What Is the Purple Bench?

A visible invitation to compassionate commerce.

Participating businesses make compassion part of the customer experience and workplace culture. The Purple Bench symbol helps residents and visitors identify places where dignity, welcome, and care are practiced intentionally.

How to Qualify

Businesses are recognized for values in action.

Empathy

Welcoming people with patience, accessibility, and respect.

Advocacy

Using local influence to support neighbors and civic good.

Social Responsibility

Choosing practices that benefit workers, customers, and community.

Sustainability

Caring for the coastal environment that sustains St. Augustine.

Collaboration

Partnering across sectors to solve local problems together.

Recognized Businesses

Celebrating local leadership.

CSA highlights businesses that model compassion in everyday operations, customer care, employee support, and community service.

Recognition listings can include business names, photos, and stories as new Purple Bench partners are welcomed.
