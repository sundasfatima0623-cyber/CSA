The Purple Bench

Compassion in Business

The Purple Bench recognizes local St. Augustine businesses that embody CSA’s core values: empathy, advocacy, social responsibility, sustainability, and collaboration.

Nominate a Business

What Is the Purple Bench?

A visible sign of community values

The Purple Bench shines a light on businesses that show compassion not just in words, but in workplace culture, community partnerships, and public presence.

How to Qualify

Values in action

Businesses are recognized for demonstrating care for employees, neighbors, and the wider community through ethical practices, sustainability, and collaboration.

Recognized Businesses

Local leaders worth celebrating

Recognition highlights businesses whose day-to-day choices help make St. Augustine more welcoming, equitable, and resilient.

Donor Tiers

Support recognition that multiplies good

  • Window Cling Sponsor Helps provide visible recognition materials for participating businesses.
  • Purple Bench with Plaque Supports a signature installation honoring compassionate business leadership.
  • Awards Luncheon Sponsorship Helps convene the community to celebrate and expand the network of recognized partners.
