Compassionate Aging St. Augustine

Aging with Dignity, Surrounded by Community

CASA is CSA's initiative to help older adults in St. Augustine age safely and joyfully in their own homes and communities through volunteer support and county partnerships.

Request Support Become a Volunteer

Our Mission

Keeping seniors connected, independent, and cared for.

CASA centers the simple truth that older neighbors belong in the rhythm of community life. With steady volunteers and trusted public partners, we help seniors remain connected to the people, places, and routines that make home feel like home.

Our tone is warm and practical: porch conversations, neighborly check-ins, rides to essentials, and thoughtful coordination when a person needs more help than one volunteer can provide.

How We Help

Neighbors helping neighbors

Invitations, introductions, and practical pathways back into neighborhood life.

For Seniors

Request support

Older adults, family members, caregivers, and neighbors may reach out to begin a conversation about what kind of support would help someone feel safer, more connected, and less alone.

  • Share the senior's name, neighborhood, and best contact information.
  • Tell us what support is needed now and what has been tried already.
  • A CASA volunteer coordinator will follow up with next steps and referrals.
Request Support

For Volunteers

Give your time

Volunteers offer the gift of presence: a ride, a phone call, a porch visit, or help linking a neighbor to the right resource. CASA welcomes patient listeners, careful drivers, organizers, and advocates.

  • Complete a volunteer interest form and orientation.
  • Choose roles that fit your availability and comfort level.
  • Serve with care, boundaries, and respect for each senior's independence.
Become a Volunteer

County Partnership

Working with St. Johns County to fill gaps in senior services.

CASA collaborates with St. Johns County and community providers to understand where older adults are falling through the cracks. Together, we coordinate volunteer care with public resources so seniors receive more complete support.

Identify Needs

Listen to seniors, families, and county partners to understand practical gaps.

Coordinate Services

Connect neighborly support with available programs, transportation, and referrals.

Advocate for Dignity

Lift up the voices of older residents in planning, policy, and community action.
