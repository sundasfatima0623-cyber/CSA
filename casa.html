CASA | Compassionate Aging St. Augustine

Aging with Dignity, Surrounded by Community

CASA helps older adults in St. Augustine age safely and joyfully in their own homes and communities through volunteer support, caring relationships, and partnership with St. Johns County.

Our Mission

Keeping seniors connected, independent, and cared for

CASA is grounded in the conviction that older adults should be able to remain in the places that hold their stories, routines, and relationships. We help strengthen the circle of support around seniors so home can continue to feel safe, familiar, and life-giving.

The tone of CASA is warm and reassuring: neighbors helping neighbors, porch by porch, visit by visit, conversation by conversation.

How We Help

Practical support that protects dignity

Volunteer home visits

Friendly check-ins and companionship that reduce isolation and strengthen trust.

Transportation assistance

Help getting to appointments, errands, and meaningful community activities.

Community connection

Support identifying social opportunities, local resources, and neighbor-to-neighbor encouragement.

County coordination

Working alongside St. Johns County to fill gaps and connect seniors with the services already available.
