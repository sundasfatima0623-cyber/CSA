Aging with Dignity, Surrounded by Community

CASA is Compassionate St. Augustine’s initiative to help older adults age safely and joyfully in their own homes and neighborhoods.

Request Support Become a Volunteer

Our Mission

We keep seniors connected, independent, and cared for by weaving together local relationships, trusted volunteers, and practical support systems.

How We Help

  • Volunteer home visits and neighbor check-ins
  • Transportation assistance for medical and essential trips
  • Community connection to local events and social support
  • Coordination with county resources to fill service gaps quickly

For Seniors

If you or a loved one needs help staying safe, connected, and active, CASA can help map out the right support. We listen first, then match each request with compassionate local help.

For Volunteers

A little time can make a big difference. CASA volunteers can assist with friendly visits, rides, and check-ins to ensure no elder in our community feels alone.

County Partnership

CASA works with St. Johns County to identify and close gaps in senior services, making sure support reaches people where and when they need it most.
