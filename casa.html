New Initiative CASA

Aging with Dignity,
Surrounded by Community

Compassionate Aging St. Augustine — helping older adults live safely and joyfully in the homes and neighborhoods they love, with the support of neighbors who care.

Our Mission

Keeping Seniors Connected, Independent, and Cared For

CASA — Compassionate Aging St. Augustine — is CSA's newest initiative, born from a simple but powerful belief: every older adult deserves to age with dignity, surrounded by community, in the place they call home.

St. Augustine has a vibrant and growing senior population. Many want to age in place — staying in the neighborhoods they know, near the people they love. CASA is here to make that possible through volunteer support, community connection, and smart partnerships with St. Johns County.

Our approach is neighbor-to-neighbor: ordinary people offering ordinary acts of care that make an extraordinary difference in an older adult's life.

How We Help

Four Ways CASA Shows Up

CASA volunteers provide practical, heartfelt support across four key areas — meeting seniors where they are and helping them thrive.

🏡

Volunteer Home Visits

Regular friendly visits from trained CASA volunteers provide companionship, a watchful eye, and the reassurance that comes from knowing someone genuinely cares. Isolation is the silent epidemic — our visits break it.

🚗

Transportation Assistance

CASA volunteers provide rides to medical appointments, grocery stores, community events, and visits with friends and family. Mobility is independence — we help seniors maintain both.

🤝

Community Connection

We connect seniors to events, social programs, faith communities, and civic life in St. Augustine. Staying engaged with community isn't a luxury — it's essential to health and happiness.

🏛️

County Coordination

CASA works in close partnership with St. Johns County Elder Services to identify gaps in care, coordinate resources, and ensure no senior falls through the cracks of the social safety net.

Who CASA Serves & Who Makes It Possible

🏠 For Seniors

Are you or a loved one an older adult in St. Augustine who could use a friendly visitor, help getting to appointments, or connection to community services?

CASA is here for you. Our services are free and provided by caring, trained community volunteers. Requesting support is simple and confidential.

How to Request Support

1

Call or email CSA to let us know you'd like CASA support

2

A CASA coordinator will contact you to learn about your needs

3

We match you with a trained volunteer and get started

County Partnership

Working with St. Johns County

🏛️

Filling the Gaps Together

CASA works in active partnership with St. Johns County Elder Services to identify where senior needs exceed available public resources — and then we fill those gaps with compassionate volunteer support.

This public-nonprofit collaboration ensures that seniors receive coordinated, comprehensive care rather than fragmented, duplicated, or missed services. By working alongside county systems rather than in isolation, CASA maximizes its impact for the seniors who need us most.
