Gift Gardens

Growing Food,
Growing Community

Youth and adult volunteers cultivate compassion one harvest at a time — feeding families across St. Augustine in partnership with EPIC-CURE.

Our Mission

From Seed to Table to Community

Gift Gardens is Compassionate St. Augustine's community garden initiative, rooted in the belief that growing food together grows community together. Our gardens are tended by youth and adult volunteers who give their time, energy, and care so that families in need don't go hungry.

All produce is distributed by our partner organization EPIC-CURE, which works tirelessly to combat food insecurity throughout St. Augustine and St. Johns County.

Whether you're an experienced gardener or have never held a trowel, there's a place for you in Gift Gardens. All are welcome, all are needed.

How It Works

From Volunteer to Harvest

1

Sign Up to Volunteer

Register online or at a CSA event. Volunteers of all ages and skill levels are welcomed into the gardens.

2

Tend the Gardens

Join scheduled planting, weeding, and watering sessions. Youth volunteers earn service hours; adult mentors share knowledge and friendship.

3

Harvest with Purpose

When produce is ready, we harvest as a team — fresh vegetables, fruits, and herbs collected for distribution.

4

EPIC-CURE Distributes

Our partner EPIC-CURE receives the harvest and ensures it reaches families experiencing food insecurity throughout the region.

Current Garden Locations

Four Gardens, One Community

Our gardens are spread across St. Augustine, bringing fresh food and community connection to neighborhoods throughout the city.

🌿

West Augustine Garden

Our largest garden serving the heart of West Augustine. Youth volunteers from local schools tend raised beds of seasonal vegetables and herbs, building skills and friendships year-round.

📍 West Augustine Neighborhood

☀️

MLK Avenue Garden

Located near CSA's home base on Martin Luther King Avenue, this garden is a visible symbol of our commitment to the community. Open to neighbors for volunteering every Saturday morning.

📍 147 MLK Ave, St. Augustine

🌻

Lighthouse Park Garden

Nestled near the historic lighthouse district, this garden specializes in native Florida plants alongside food crops, serving as a beautiful demonstration site for sustainable growing practices.

📍 Lighthouse District, St. Augustine

🌾

Lincolnville Heritage Garden

Rooted in the historic Lincolnville neighborhood, this garden honors the community's rich history while growing food for today's residents. Intergenerational mentoring is at the heart of everything we do here.

📍 Lincolnville, St. Augustine

Our Partner

Working Together with EPIC-CURE

EPIC-CURE: Fighting Food Insecurity

EPIC-CURE is CSA's trusted distribution partner and a vital force in combating hunger across St. Johns County. When our gardens harvest, EPIC-CURE ensures that every pound of produce reaches the families who need it most.

Together, Gift Gardens and EPIC-CURE form a compassionate supply chain: volunteers grow, EPIC-CURE distributes, and families are nourished — all powered by community love.

🤝 EPIC-CURE
🌿 Gift Gardens
💚 St. Johns County
