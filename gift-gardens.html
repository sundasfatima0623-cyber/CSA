Growing Food, Growing Community

Community gardens tended by youth and adult volunteers across St. Augustine — every harvest helps feed a neighbor.

Volunteer in the Gardens Meet Our Partner
Our Approach

Gardens that nourish more than soil

Every Gift Garden is a small ecosystem of generosity. Youth and adult volunteers plant, tend, and harvest fresh produce that is then distributed by our partner EPIC-CURE to neighbors experiencing food insecurity in St. Augustine.

More than vegetables grow here — friendships, skills, and a deeper sense of belonging take root in every bed.

How It Works

From seed to neighbor

  • 1. PlantSeasonal crops are chosen with our garden leads and local growers.
  • 2. TendVolunteers of all ages learn to weed, water, and care for each bed.
  • 3. HarvestProduce is picked at peak freshness during weekly harvest days.
  • 4. ShareEPIC-CURE distributes harvests to families across St. Augustine.
Volunteer

Lend a hand. Grow a community.

No experience needed — just a willingness to dig in. We welcome individuals, families, scout troops, faith groups, and school groups for one-time service days or ongoing stewardship.

  • Weekday and weekend shifts available
  • Tools, gloves, and guidance provided
  • Service-learning hours available for students

Our Partner

EPIC-CURE

EPIC-CURE rescues, prepares, and distributes fresh food across St. Augustine and St. Johns County. Every Gift Garden harvest flows through their network — ensuring our produce reaches the neighbors who need it most.
