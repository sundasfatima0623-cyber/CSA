What is Good Trouble?

Inspired by courageous civic leaders, Good Trouble is CSA's advocacy arm focused on compassion-driven policy and accountability. We help neighbors raise their voices, understand local systems, and stay engaged over the long term.

Current Campaigns

  • Housing affordability listening sessions
  • Compassionate public safety alternatives
  • Neighborhood resilience and climate preparedness

Coalition Partners

  • Neighborhood associations
  • Local nonprofit organizations
  • Faith communities and civic advocates
