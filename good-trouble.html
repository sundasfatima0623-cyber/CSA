What is Good Trouble?

Grassroots action with an open hand and a clear voice

Good Trouble convenes neighbors who believe compassion belongs in civic life. The group researches local issues, builds relationships with decision-makers, and turns concern into organized public action.

Current Campaigns

Listening, learning, mobilizing

Current efforts include attending public meetings, organizing educational forums, amplifying resident voices, and partnering with aligned organizations to move urgent community priorities forward.

Coalition Partners

Relationships that make action possible

Good Trouble works in coalition with community-based organizations, clergy, educators, neighborhood leaders, service providers, and public officials who are committed to practical and compassionate change.
