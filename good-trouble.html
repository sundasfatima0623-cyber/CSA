What We Stand For

What is Good Trouble?

Good Trouble is CSA’s advocacy arm — a coalition of residents, leaders, organizations, and elected officials who organize for change rooted in compassion. We believe meaningful progress comes from listening deeply, mobilizing locally, and acting persistently.

Our Action Areas

Three urgent priorities

🏠

Affordable Housing

Advocating for housing policies that keep working families rooted in St. Augustine.

🏕️

Homelessness Crisis

Building dignified, compassionate responses to the homelessness crisis in our community.

🌿

Environment

Protecting the coastline, waterways, and natural beauty that define St. Augustine.

In Motion

Current Campaigns

  • Housing ForwardWorking with city and county leaders to expand affordable workforce housing options.
  • Shelter & DignityConvening service providers and faith communities around long-term solutions to homelessness.
  • Coastline CareMobilizing volunteers for cleanups and pressing for stronger coastal protections.
  • Voices HeardTown halls and listening sessions that put residents in front of decision-makers.
